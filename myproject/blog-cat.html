How to Take Care of Your Cat’s Mental Health?

Cats are often seen as independent and emotionally distant, but they experience stress, boredom, and anxiety just like humans. Mental stimulation is essential for their overall well-being.

Signs of Mental Stress in Cats

  • Over-grooming
  • Hiding excessively
  • Sudden aggression
  • Loss of appetite

Ways to Support Your Cat

Provide interactive toys, vertical climbing spaces, routine feeding schedules, and consistent affection. Cats thrive in predictable environments.

By paying attention to their emotional signals, you ensure they live not just longer — but happier lives.
