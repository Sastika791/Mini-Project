All About Sugar Gliders

Ever hear an exotic pet parent enthusiastically explaining how sugar gliders make wonderful pets and think, “What is a sugar glider, exactly?”

Sugar gliders are small, nocturnal marsupials native to Australia and Indonesia. They are named for their love of sweet foods and their ability to glide through the air using a membrane between their limbs.

Are They Good Pets?

They can be affectionate and playful, but they require special care, attention, and a proper habitat setup.

Important Considerations

  • They are social and should not live alone.
  • They require a large vertical cage.
  • Their diet must be carefully balanced.
  • They are nocturnal and active at night.

Is It Right for You?

Sugar gliders are not beginner pets. Research thoroughly before making the commitment. When cared for properly, they can form strong bonds with their owners.

Exotic pets require exotic responsibility. Make sure you're prepared before bringing one home.
